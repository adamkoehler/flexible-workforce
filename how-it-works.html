How It Works

Flexible WorkForce has developed technology and a model strategy that is far superior to any existing 3rd Party Administrator servicing the customized delivery & logistics industry.

Our State-of-the-Art technology platform provides your business with an end-to-end solution to establish automated workflows and processes across all Flexible Workforce Management activities. All with full access, dashboards, and visibility to recruiting, on-boarding, compliance, payments, reporting, driver negotiation, and access to the Driver Marketplace to find and seamlessly on-board contracted and ready-to-go drivers.

  • RECRUIT

    Eliminate the time and hassles of looking for qualified drivers though our Driver Marketplace or we can recruit for you on a geographic or specific criteria basis using our automated vendor recruiting and applicant technology.

  • SCREEN

    Expedite vendor driver screening with best in class, extremely competitive, and instant result MVR checks, background checks, and drug tests. “Daily” MVR record checks and alerts are also available to further help minimize risk.

  • VERIFY

    Streamline the applicant process using our powerful verification tools to review vendor profile credentials and compliance data to seamlessly on-board the perfect driver for your business.

  • ON-BOARD

    Simplify and Automate vendor driver on-boarding with our streamlined electronic and mobile friendly enrollment process. Complete with electronic document signature, validation checks, and real-time enrollment notifications.

  • MANAGE

    Eliminate the costly back office processes required to manage driver documents, compliance, negotiate rates, and pay vendor settlements to drivers.

  • Pay

    Remove the administrative burdens and processes of payments to vendors, drivers, agents, and partners by leveraging our on-demand, weekly, bi-weekly, or semi-monthly settlement processing, and payment services. Settlement data integrations are available to securely and seamlessly send settlement data direct from your operating system.
